Privacy Policy for Focus+

Last updated: March 2025

Focus+ ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our focus timer app.

Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device: Your focus sessions, achievements, streaks, goals, and settings are stored only on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data to our servers.

Advertising: The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version)
  • Usage data related to ad performance

This data is used to show relevant ads and measure ad effectiveness. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Calendar: If you choose to sync planned focus sessions with Apple Calendar, the app accesses your calendar to create and manage events. Calendar data is processed locally and synced with Apple's Calendar service. We do not store or transmit your calendar data to our servers.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the app
  • To display ads (via AdMob)
  • To sync planned sessions with your calendar (when you enable this feature)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your local app data (sessions, achievements) is not shared. AdMob may share data as described in Google's privacy policy.

Your Choices

  • You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Advertising)
  • You can deny calendar access when prompted; the app will work without it.
